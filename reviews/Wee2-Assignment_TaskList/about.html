Purpose:

This site is for helping people organize and managable their daily tasks lists. Tasks can be assigned to a person or persons as well as an area to describe detailed information of needs doing. Once the task is marked as complete it is moved over to the completed list. As such it helps give a clear view of what was completed and what still needs to be done.
