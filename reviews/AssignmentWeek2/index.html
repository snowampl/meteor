This is Fido. His owner's name is Mr. John. He is a nice, lovable and playful dog who is very loyal to his owner. He loves children. Every morning, Fido and Mr. John sit on the porch to make sure that the neighborhood kids reach school safely. Two weeks ago there was a very bad rain storm. The storm caused a big tree branch to fall on Fido's doghouse. Mr. John cannot afford to build Fido a new doghouse so we kids decided to build one for him.
