A Home for Fido

  1. Home
  2. About

     A home for Fido was created to show people around the world that pets matter.  When we , the children who live on Brandon Street, saw that our friend Fido lost his home in the storm, we decided to help.  Pets are members of our family.  No family member should have to sleep out in the cold or rain.  No one is too young or too old to help a pet in need.
